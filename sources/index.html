POLYGON is an armchair born from the modernist seating typology with small dimensions. It meets the functional parameters, but due to its size and immobility it does not take up unnecessary areas.

Polygon Collection


Manufacturer: PROSTORIA.

With the change in perspective, the triangles that define the orthogonal side view of the chair transform into hexagons and other complex polygons. The result is a product that looks familiar, even a little dated, but is at the same time decidedly modern. The construction of bent steel rods further accentuates the duality of the polygon.
